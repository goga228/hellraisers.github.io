ASUS presents the notebook ROG G501

  • 26 May 2015
Powerful, ultrathin gaming notebook with the Intel Core i7 processor and a NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960M graphics card and the upgraded Hyper Cool technology.

The Asus Republic of Gamers has presented the new gaming notebook ROG G501. It is just 20.6mm and 2.06kg that makes it the thinnest gaming laptops in its class. 

ROG G501 can boast the latest 4th-generation Intel® Core™ i7 processor and a NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960M graphics card that guarantee the breathtaking levels of performance and the gaming experience. Hyper Cool technology provides the stable operation of all components through the cooling system which uses two heat sink fans.

ROG G501 has a 15.6-inch, 4K/IPS display with Ultra-HD resolution and high pixel density. It brings an immersive viewing experience. 

Portable gaming notebook

ROG G501 is one of the thinnest gaming laptops. The stylish, covered in red colors ergonomic backlit keyboard with special keys gives the laptop a gaming character. 

 

Exclusive Hyper Cool technology

Thanks to the exclusive Hyper Cool technology ROG G501 has become the quietest laptop for gamers. The cooling system uses two heat sink fans and copper heatsinks with heatpipes. The processor and video card are cooled independently, it gives an incredible gaming performance without much noise. 

 

Uncompromised Performance

The latest 4th-generation Intel® Core™ i7 processor became a heart of the ROG G501 and provided the opportunity to play the latest games. NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960M with the on-board GDDR5 memory up to 4GB is responsible for smooth and stable performance at processing photos and video editing.

Due to the high data-transfer speeds (around 1 400 Mb/s), PCI Express® x4 solid state drive is capable to accelerate the operation system loading more than twice in comparison to solid state drives with SATA interface and 16 times faster than common hard drives. Thunderbolt technology provides a single-cable data and graphics interface with speeds of up to 10Gbit/s – twice the bandwidth of USB 3.0.

 

The amazing image and impressive sound

ROG G501 boasts a 15.6-inch, 4K/UHD display with 8.2-million pixels. Due to IPS matrix with wide viewing angles (up to 178 degrees), the image hardly undergoes any color distortion when you change the angle. Exclusive ASUS Splendid Technology provides an excellent color rendition, high detailed textures and a bright, sharp picture.  The display of ROG G501 will perfectly suit for watching hd movies, surfing the internet, and editing photos and videos. On-board HD-camera gives the high quality video calls even in low light. 

Bang & Olufsen ICEpower Sound System together with ASUS SonicMaster Technology, implemented in ROG G501, will satisfy the owners with high sound quality. In addition to that, there is AudioWizard function, which is used for audio settings, where you may choose one of five variants of sound system’s work, perfectly done for every type of apps.  

 

Pricing and Availability

The notebook ROG G501 is available now for $1,699.
